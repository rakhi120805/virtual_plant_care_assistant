🌿 Quick Plant Care Tips

  • ☀️ Light: Most plants thrive in bright, indirect sunlight.
  • 💧 Watering: Water only when the top inch of soil feels dry.
  • 💨 Humidity: Mist tropical plants or group them for moisture.
